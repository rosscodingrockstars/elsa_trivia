The directions pop up,
You'll click play to start the trivia game.
One question comes up on the screen.
You have to choose between four answers by clicking on the answer you believe is correct.
Then you click submit to confirm your answer.
A gif pops up saying the correct phrase.
If you got it wrong, it says the correct phrase. 
If you got it right, it says you got the answer correct.
Then you click the button that says "Continue to next question".
It repeats this process until you have answered every question.
At the end of the trivia, it will say how many answers you got correct out of all of them.
